The Method

Not answers.
Questions that reveal.

Most AI tutors rob you of the thinking. Socratic AI refuses. Every interaction is engineered to make the insight yours.

01 🎯

You ask a question

Ask anything about Data Science or Machine Learning — concepts, code, theory. The AI detects your intent and adjusts its approach.

02 🧠

The AI thinks first

Internally, the model generates chain-of-thought reasoning via <think> blocks — deliberating on the best guiding question before responding.

03 ❓

You receive a question

Never the answer. Always a question that leads you there. Fine-tuned on 307 Socratic conversations so this is learned behavior, not a prompt.

04 💡

You discover the answer

The insight becomes yours — not borrowed. Research shows self-generated understanding is retained far longer than passive reading.
